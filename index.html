About the Cosmos

The cosmos is a vast and mysterious space filled with stars, galaxies, planets, and endless wonders. It connects us to the universe and inspires curiosity beyond imagination. This website explores cosmic beauty through visuals and stories, capturing the magic of nature and the skies above.

Quick Cosmic Facts

  • The observable universe is about 93 billion light-years in diameter.
  • Our solar system is located in the Orion Arm of the Milky Way galaxy.
  • There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on Earth.
  • The closest galaxy to us is the Andromeda Galaxy, 2.5 million light-years away.
  • Black holes have such strong gravity that not even light can escape them.
